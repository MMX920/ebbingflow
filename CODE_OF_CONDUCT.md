# Code of Conduct

## 1. Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming, harassment-free, and inclusive
community for everyone.

## 2. Expected Behavior

- be respectful and constructive
- assume good intent and discuss ideas, not people
- provide actionable technical feedback
- accept responsibility and correct mistakes quickly
- prioritize user safety and project integrity

## 3. Unacceptable Behavior

- harassment, hate speech, or discrimination
- personal attacks, threats, or intimidation
- doxxing or sharing private information without consent
- spam, trolling, or repeated bad-faith disruption
- intentionally misleading security or data-handling claims

## 4. Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for interpretation and enforcement of this
Code of Conduct.

Maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate, including:

- warning
- temporary restriction
- permanent ban from project spaces

## 5. Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it to maintainers.
Please include links, screenshots, and timestamps when possible.

All reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with reasonable privacy.

## 6. Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including issue trackers,
pull requests, discussions, and related community channels.
